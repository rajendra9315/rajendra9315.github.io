Privacy Policy

Meta & Telegram Ads — How we handle client data

1. Introduction

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information while running Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and Telegram ad campaigns for our clients.

2. Information We Collect

  • Basic business information (company name, website, social profiles)
  • Campaign data (audience, target locations, ad performance)
  • Landing page or website analytics, if applicable

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Create and manage ad campaigns on Meta and Telegram platforms.
  • Optimize campaigns for better performance and ROI.
  • Provide reports and insights to clients for transparency.

4. Data Protection

We follow strict security measures to protect your information:

  • Access to client data is limited to authorized personnel only.
  • All accounts and passwords are stored securely.
  • We do not share your personal or business data with third parties without consent.

5. Cookies & Tracking

When we set up campaigns, we may implement Meta Pixel, Telegram tracking, or other analytics tools on your website/landing pages. This helps:

  • Measure campaign performance.
  • Retarget audiences for better results.
  • Optimize ad spend efficiently.

6. Your Rights

  • You can ask us to delete your campaign data if needed.
  • We will provide transparency on any tracking implemented on your behalf.
